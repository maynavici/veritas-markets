Trading Constitution

Rules that don’t negotiate.

The vault stores your approved trading laws, setups, invalidations, defensive rules, and non-negotiables. During live market hours, the constitution should be read-only.

Capital Protection Law

The account is more important than the trade. One opportunity means nothing. Surviving the month matters.

  • Max risk per trade: 0.5%
  • Stop after two emotional trades
  • No recovery lot size

Execution Law

Every trade must have a written thesis, defined invalidation, target, risk, and post-trade review.

  • No entry without thesis
  • No SL movement from fear
  • No chasing after missing the clean entry

Emotional Law

Bad mental state equals no trading. The market will still be here tomorrow.

  • Angry = locked
  • Bored = locked
  • Greedy after wins = caution

Session Law

Trade only during your approved operational window. Outside that: review, journal, and prepare.

  • London review allowed
  • New York execution window
  • Friday late session caution

News Law

Major news can destroy clean execution. If news is near, the system demands a manual check.

  • CPI
  • NFP
  • FOMC and rate decisions
  • Central bank speeches

Review Law

The trade is not finished after exit. The trade is finished after review.

  • Screenshot before/during/after
  • Emotion recorded
  • Grade assigned
  • Mistake cost updated
